WORK IN PROGGRESS : Shining
Shining is An all-in-one, DIY, accessible and secure system for electrodeposition on small to medium-sized parts.

what is electrodeposition ?
Electroplating or electroforming consists of using electricity to depose a thin layer of metal (copper, nickel, palladium, gold…) on a conductive piece. It is used in industry, jewellery and electronics to give metallic and non metallic pieces the characteristic of a metal without sacrificing cost or strength

What am I proposing ?
I want to design and manufacture a all in one diy electroplating machine that answers those constraint :
- can be used without an external generator
- consistent result even with minimum knowledge of the practice
- the least amount of contact between the acid and the user
- fully made with only generic parts and a 3D printer
- easily scalable for bigger parts
